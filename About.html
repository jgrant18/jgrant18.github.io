About

“Jackie Grant is an emerging multimedia artist and writer. She has a particular interest in the intersection of popular culture and the human experience. Her current writing project looks back at the film of her childhood to better understand how it influenced her as an adult, her tastes, hangups, beliefs, and humour. This site is designed to shift your interaction with imagery and text, to create an interactive and temporary personal gallery.
